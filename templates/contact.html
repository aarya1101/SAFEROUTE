FAQ

Common Questions

Is anonymous reporting truly anonymous?
Yes. SafeRoute's anonymous reporting system does not collect any personally identifiable information. No account, no name, no IP logging, and no location data beyond what you voluntarily provide in the report itself.
How does the AI prediction system work?
Our models are trained on verified historical records, time-of-day patterns, and environmental factors. The system maps risk zones dynamically, enabling proactive safe route generation for women traveling at any hour.
How do I register as a law enforcement officer?
Officer registration requires secure verification of your badge ID, station assignment, and a liveness video check. Visit the registration page, select "Verified Officer," and complete the identity protocol.
Who has access to submitted reports?
Reports are accessible exclusively to verified law enforcement officers assigned to the relevant zone and platform administrators for moderation. Report data is strictly encrypted and never shared with third parties.
